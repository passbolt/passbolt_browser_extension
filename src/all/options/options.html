Autofill

Control how passbolt fills credentials when you launch a resource from the quickaccess.

Autofill on launch

When you launch a credential from the quickaccess search results, navigate to its saved website and autofill the login form.

The form is filled, not submitted, and only when the loaded page's origin matches the saved URL. This setting is stored locally in this browser only — it is never sent to your passbolt server.
